Linked Data Crawl 2014

We experimented with the Linked Data Crawl generated in 2014 by Schmachtenberg et al. (ISWC 2014 publication).

Contexts in N-Quads

Quality of N-Quads

Predicates

By looking at the set of different predicates in the links, we classified them depending on their purpose. We identified quadruples that: (Data set level)

  • provide metadata about a data set or a vocabulary (Schema level)
  • define a vocabulary term (property or class)
  • map vocabulary terms (Entity level)
  • classify an entity
  • connect two identical entities
  • connect two similar entities
  • connect two related two entities, either with a domain-specific relationship (e.\,g.\texttt{foaf:knows}), or a more general relationship (e.\,g.\ \texttt{rdfs:seeAlso})
